JDM (full name Japanese domestic market or Japanese domestic model) translated into Chinese means the domestic market in Japan. Simply put, it refers to the "exclusive" models in the Japanese market, such as the Civic Type-R, Nissan R34 GTR, Toyota 2000GT, etc.
